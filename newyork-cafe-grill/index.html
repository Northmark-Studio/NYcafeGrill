Our place

A neighborhood deli, done right.

Every morning starts the same way here: ovens on, bagels in, coffee brewing. Fresh-baked bagels, hot and cold sandwiches, wraps, and salads — made the way a good deli should make them.

We're a family-run spot on Inman Ave, serving the Edison neighborhood since 2021. Come in for breakfast, grab lunch to go, or order ahead — either way, you're getting it fresh.
